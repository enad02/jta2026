Framework Architecture

The framework is organised into three document series. Foundations define the academy model. Teaching Standards define delivery expectations. Tutor Development defines progression, reflection, mentoring, and certification.

JF

Foundations

Core philosophy and instructional model.

JTS

Teaching Standards

Operational teaching and communication standards.

JTD

Tutor Development

Reflection, residency, mentoring, and sign-off standards.

How the framework is used

Tutors use these documents to understand teaching expectations, lesson standards, reflection processes, and certification requirements. Mentors use the same framework for observation, coaching, feedback, and consistency across the academy.
